Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Eazy Ride respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does (and does not) collect, how it is used, and your rights.

Information we do not collect

Eazy Ride does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, ads, tracking technologies, or cloud services to process your data.

Information the app uses locally

  • Bluetooth: Used to connect to your smart trainer via the FTMS protocol. Connection information remains on your device only.
  • Location: Required by Android to perform Bluetooth scanning. The app does not record or transmit your location.
  • GPX routes: Any routes you import are stored locally on your device and never uploaded.

Sharing of information

Eazy Ride does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

Your rights

Since no personal information is collected, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. You remain fully in control of your data on your own device.
