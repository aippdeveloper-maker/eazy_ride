What it does

Eazy Ride lets you import your own routes as GPX files and connects to your smart trainer via Bluetooth using the FTMS protocol. Start pedaling and watch your position progress along the route while monitoring cadence, power (watts), speed, and elevation on an interactive map and elevation panel.

Why I built it

I wanted to ride my routes on a trainer and benchmark how quickly I could reach, for example, my office—then track improvement over time. Existing apps are great, but felt heavy for this simple goal. Eazy Ride keeps it focused: no subscriptions, no social overhead—just your route and your effort.
